Platform ecosystems

Multi-Platform SVOD & Prime Video Channel Launch

Problem

Launching a streaming product across many device ecosystems and onto a partner platform with a strict certification spec. Needed the app testable end to end across device ecosystems and a delivery pipeline that could meet Amazon's partner SLAs.

Methodology

Took a streaming app to internal beta across 7 device ecosystems — fully testable end to end, capable of payment and playback across thousands of titles. Led technical certification through Amazon's partner onboarding program: built MovieLabs-spec XML generation for automated metadata delivery; normalized internal assets and metadata to partner spec; closed gaps against Amazon's certification spec across artwork, timed text, and A/V requirements; proved the delivery pipeline could meet Amazon's required partner SLAs.

Impact

Launched a live Prime Video Channel.
